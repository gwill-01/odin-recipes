Shrimp Tostadas

Description

Shrimp and avacado tostadas with cilantro, tomato, cucumbers, and red onion. Excellent when servered with a side of black beans.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Avacado
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber
  • Cilantro
  • Red Onion
  • Lime Juice
  • Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables and put into a bowl.
  2. Juice 1 lemon and 1 lime and add to vegetable mixture.
  3. Cook shrimp.
  4. Finely chop shrimp and add to vegetable mixture.
  5. Mix and salt to taste.
